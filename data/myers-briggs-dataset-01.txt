Sources of the data used in this Myers-Briggs dataset.

# PPT, PPF & PPM from: https://www.myersbriggs.org/my-mbti-personality-type/my-mbti-results/how-frequent-is-my-type.htm
# plus: https://www.quora.com/What-is-the-frequency-distribution-of-different-MBTI-personality-types/answer/Rahul-Tiwari-160

# The same data is also available (with a subscription) from https://www.statisticbrain.com/myers-briggs-statistics/

# upper and lower bounds from: https://www.capt.org/mbti-assessment/estimated-frequencies.htm?bhjs=0

Columns:

D1 = objective deduction (I or E)

D2 = subjective deduction (N or S)

D3 = subjective induction (F or T)

D4 objective induction (P or J)

Pair = relates each type to another type using a rotational or inversion symmetry (this is experimental / exploratory)

PPT = percentage prevalence for the total population
PPTL = PPT lower bound
PPTU = PPT upper bound

PPF = percentage prevalence for the female population
PPFL = PPF lower bound
PPFU = PPF upper bound

PPM = percentage prevalence for the male population
PPML = PPM lower bound
PPMU = PPM upper bound
